Swimming Techniques

  1. Freestyle
    • Fastest stroke
    • Uses flutter kicks
    • Great for endurance
  2. Backstroke
    • Relaxing stroke
    • Helps improve posture
    • Uses alternating arm motion
  3. Butterfly
    • Most challenging stroke
    • Builds upper body strength
    • Requires simultaneous arm movement
  4. Breaststroke
    • Slow but efficient
    • Ideal for beginners
    • Focuses on a frog-like kick
